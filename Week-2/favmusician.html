John Mayer

John Mayer is an American singer-songwriter, guitarist, and producer who was born on October 16, 1977, in Bridgeport, Connecticut. He first gained popularity in the early 2000s with his debut album Room for Squares, which included the hit singles "Your Body Is a Wonderland" and "No Such Thing." Since then, he has released several successful albums and collaborated with many other musicians, including the Grateful Dead and Alicia Keys.
